Final Project

For our final project, we will be working in groups of 4. The members are JiaLe, WeiErn, Fraser, Diyana.

We wanted to create a RGB lamp that can change color everytime the button is pressed. The colors are red, green, blue, violet, yellow, light blue, and white

As for the work distributions, I was in-charge of dealing with 3D printing while JiaLe was in-charge of coding of the circuit. Meanwhile, WeiErn and Fraser was in-charge of doing laser-cutting for the product.
